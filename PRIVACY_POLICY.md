# Privacy Policy for lasDoscas - YouTube™ Dual Subtitles

Last updated: 2026-06-30

## 1. Information Collection and Use
**lasDoscas YouTube Dual Subtitles** is a privacy-first browser extension. We believe that your private data belongs to you. 
- **No Personal Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, log, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data.
- **Local Processing:** All extension configurations (such as font size, background opacity, and language choices) are processed and stored locally on your device via Chrome's native storage API (`chrome.storage.local`).

## 2. Third-Party Services
Our extension interacts with YouTube to fetch native subtitle data and may communicate with official translation APIs to provide translation services. No user-identifying data is shared with these services during this process.

## 3. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub Issues page.
